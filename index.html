Quick bio

Hello! I'm a software engineer who has been working on commercial projects since 2010. Along the way I have been very lucky to learn from outstanding people and work with them on exciting projects:

  • designed architecture of white label service which establish interoperability with EETS Toll Charges Domains
  • helped to scale a platform for gathering and data analysis from social media sites
  • helped to redesign and scale data processing pipeline for biotech start-up (GitHub)
  • helped to deliver outdoor and indoor lighting control system
  • helped to deliver access control and visitor management system
During my career I have been mostly focused on engineering and scaling of distributed data-intensive systems. In the past years I also got more opportunities to apply bits of my university background (economics, modeling, systems engineering, business continuity) to design, improve efficiency and reliability of large scale systems. If you want to get more info, here is my CV.
